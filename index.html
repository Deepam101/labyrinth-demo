Presented by Spring Fest,Labyrinth 10.0 is an Online Mind Maze which can be quite confusing and frustrating. Answers can always be extractd by finding,following and linking all hidden clues and hints presented in your screen or webpage. Clues can be kept anywhere in the page like an image, url etc. Using other possible senses apart from common-sense may be helpful. Remember!
